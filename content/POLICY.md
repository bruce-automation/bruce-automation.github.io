# Content policy (repo)

## Principles
- No fake citations, no invented numbers.
- Claims that look like facts must be sourced.
- Prefer first-hand tests (screenshots, step-by-step) when possible.
- Avoid YMYL advice (medical/legal/financial) unless clearly framed and sourced.

## Disclosures
- If an article contains affiliate links, add a short disclosure near the first affiliate link.

## Forbidden
- Guaranteed earnings claims.
- Misleading screenshots.
